Shiro

Description

A lovely addition to budena and many of Horn of Africans favourite stews: shiro!
Generally known as an economical dish, you will be full on a budget. It can also be eaten with other (flat)breads.

    Ingredients

  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 1135 ml of water
  • 3 jalapeños, opened up and gutted, if you are not a fan of spiciness
  • 1/2 pepper, sliced
  • 100 g shiro powder
  • salt to taste

    Steps

  1. Bake the onion. First two minutes without oil, then until golden brown.
  2. Add the tomatoes and bake until the moisture is desolved.
  3. Put some of the onion-tomato mix in the three jalapeños.
  4. Add the water and the pepper to the mixture and cook until it boils.
  5. Add the shiro powder little by little and mix it in until there are no lumps anymore. You can also use a sieve.
  6. Add salt and cook until it bubbles.
