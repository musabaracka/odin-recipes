Marqaa

Description

If you need new breakfast inspiration, look no further: marqaa will make all your doughy wishes true!
This dish is typically eaten with clarified butter and berbere powder - a spicy paprika powder that is widely used in kitchens in the Horn of Africa.

    Ingredients

  • 500 ml water
  • 250 g flour
  • 100 ml clarified butter
  • 150 g cottage cheese
  • berbere to taste
  • a pinch of salt

    Steps

  1. Cook the water with the salt and add the flour piece by piece.
  2. Continuously mix the mixture and add water if necessary. If the mixture almost has the form of a ball, you can stop.
  3. Serve the marqaa in a bowl. Make a hole in the middle and pour in some clarified butter. Add the berbere and the cottage cheese.
